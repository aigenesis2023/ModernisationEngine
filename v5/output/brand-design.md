# DESIGN.md — Brand Brief

## Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Dark, sleek, tech-forward, minimal. A modern AI/SaaS product feel with near-black backgrounds and vibrant cyan-teal accents. The atmosphere is confident, cutting-edge, and developer-oriented — precision meets polish. Clean lines, sharp contrast, and a sense of quiet power.

## Colour Palette & Roles
- **Background:** Deep near-black — the dominant canvas throughout
- **Surface:** Very dark grey, slightly lifted from background — for cards and dashboard panels
- **Primary / Accent:** Bright cyan-teal — used for primary CTA buttons, active states, and key highlights. Energetic and eye-catching against the dark backdrop.
- **Secondary:** Soft white — used for secondary buttons and text elements
- **Text — Primary:** Pure white for headlines and primary content
- **Text — Secondary:** Medium grey for body text and descriptions
- **Borders:** Very subtle dark grey lines — minimal separation between elements
- **Badge/pill accents:** Cyan-teal outlined pills for announcements and labels

## Typography Rules
- **Headlines:** Bold modern sans-serif, large scale, tight tracking. Confident and commanding. Use INTER or SPACE_GROTESK for geometric precision.
- **Body text:** Clean lightweight sans-serif, high readability on dark backgrounds. Use INTER for comfort.
- **Labels & UI:** Small, clean, uppercase tracking for navigation items and badge text.

## Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Rounded pill-shaped CTAs — primary in bright cyan-teal with white text, secondary outlined in white/grey. Subtle hover glow.
- **Cards:** Dark glass panels with very subtle borders — near-invisible elevation on near-black backgrounds. Rounded corners.
- **Navigation:** Clean dark header with logo left, text links centre, CTA button right. Minimal and professional.
- **Dashboard mockup:** Dark themed UI with data visualisations, showing the product in action — reinforces the tech-forward feel.
- **Announcement pills:** Small rounded badges with cyan outline — "Announcing: ..." style.

## Layout Principles
- Generous vertical spacing — premium breathing room
- Content centred with comfortable max-width
- Hero dominates with large bold typography and clear CTA hierarchy
- Below-the-fold shows product dashboard mockup — proof of capability
- Dark mode throughout — no light variant

## Image Treatment
- **Photographic mood:** Dark, dramatic, high-contrast
- **Colour temperature:** Cool undertones — cyan, teal, blue highlights against deep blacks
- **Composition:** Clean, tech-focused, interface-style imagery with dramatic lighting
- **Atmosphere:** Futuristic and precise — sharp focus with selective cool-toned highlights
