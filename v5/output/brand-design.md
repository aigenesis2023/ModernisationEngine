# DESIGN.md — Brand Brief

## Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Dark, sleek, tech-forward, high-contrast. The overall feel is bold and modern — a near-black background with vivid lime-green accents creating a striking, premium technology aesthetic. The atmosphere is confident, cutting-edge, and developer-oriented with subtle organic texture from diagonal light streaks.

## Colour Palette & Roles
- **Primary surface:** Deep black / near-black — the dominant background colour
- **Primary accent:** Vibrant lime green — used for CTAs, highlighted text, and interactive elements
- **Primary text:** Crisp white for headlines and body text against the dark background
- **Secondary text:** Muted gray for supporting copy and navigation links
- **Surface containers:** Dark charcoal cards with subtle borders, slightly lighter than the background
- **Trust indicators:** Soft gold/amber for award laurels and social proof elements

## Typography Rules
- **Headings:** Large-scale, bold sans-serif with tight tracking — clean and geometric
- **Body text:** Medium-weight sans-serif, comfortable reading size with good line spacing
- **Navigation:** Small, lightweight uppercase text with generous letter spacing
- **Suggested Stitch fonts:** SPACE_GROTESK for headlines, INTER for body text

## Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Rounded pill shape with lime-green fill for primary CTAs, outlined with green border for secondary actions
- **Cards:** Dark glass-like panels with subtle borders, rounded corners (12-16px), slight elevation
- **Navigation bar:** Transparent, blending with dark background, white text links, green pill CTA on right
- **Award badges:** Laurel wreath icons in muted gold with small text labels
- **Feature icons:** Contained in rounded dark panels with subtle green accents

## Layout Principles
- Generous vertical spacing with dramatic section padding
- Full-width hero with oversized typography dominating the viewport
- Centered content alignment with comfortable max-width
- Grid-based feature sections below the fold
- Diagonal light streaks add depth without distracting from content

## Image Treatment
- Dark, dramatic, low-key lighting
- Cool green-black colour temperature
- Moody atmospheric feel with deep shadows
- Abstract organic textures — flowing light streaks and subtle gradients
- Professional and futuristic mood
