Hierarchy of Controls

The fundamental framework for mitigating risk from source to individual.

Physically remove the hazard. This is the most effective control measure. If a task is not necessary, or the hazard can be removed entirely, it must be done.
Replace the hazard with a safer alternative. Use non-toxic chemicals instead of hazardous ones.

Recognising Physical Threats

Physical hazards are environmental factors that can cause harm without necessarily touching you. This includes noise, vibration, radiation, and temperature extremes.
