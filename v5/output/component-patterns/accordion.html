The Five Levels

01 ELIMINATION expand_more
Physically remove the hazard. This is the most effective control measure. If the hazard is not there, it cannot cause harm.
02 SUBSTITUTION expand_more
Replace the hazard with something less dangerous (e.g., using a water-based solvent instead of a chemical-based one).
03 ENGINEERING expand_more
Isolate people from the hazard through physical barriers, ventilation systems, or automated machine guards.
