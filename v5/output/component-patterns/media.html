Fire Extinguisher: PASS Technique

P

Pull the Pin

Break the seal and pull the safety pin.

A

Aim Low

Point the nozzle at the base of the fire.

S

Squeeze the Lever

Discharge the agent in a controlled manner.

S

Sweep Side-to-Side

Move across the base of the flames.

Pressure Gauge

Check that the needle is in the green zone before use.
