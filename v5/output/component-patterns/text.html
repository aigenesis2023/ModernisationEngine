The Philosophy of the Void

In the Obsidian Forge, we reject the notion of flat surfaces. We believe that information exists within a physical space, shaped by the tension between light and shadow. Every component you build is not a box, but a volume of energy.

This course will guide you through the technical implementation of "The Obsidian Forge" design system, focusing on depth, tonality, and the 'No-Line' rule of interface construction.
