Glossary of Terms

Hazard

A potential source of harm or adverse health effect on a person or persons.

Risk

The likelihood that a person may be harmed or suffer adverse health effects if exposed to a hazard.

Near Miss

An unplanned event that did not result in injury, illness, or damage—but had the potential to do so.

PPE

Personal Protective Equipment designed to minimize exposure to hazards that cause serious injuries.

SDS

Safety Data Sheet. Document that provides health and safety information about products and chemicals.

Incident

An occurrence, condition, or situation arising in the course of work that resulted in or could have resulted in injuries.
