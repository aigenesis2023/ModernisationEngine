Halation

The visual blooming effect caused by 100% white text on pure black backgrounds. Avoided in our system by using On-Surface Variant (#ADAAAA).

Ghost Border

A stroke of 20% opacity using Outline Variant, used only when accessibility mandates a boundary that tonal shifts cannot provide.

Tonal Nesting

The hierarchy of surfaces where lighter shades of gray/black represent physical closeness to the user.
