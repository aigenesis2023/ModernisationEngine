The Warehouse Incident

A case study in root cause analysis.

01. Setting

Busy Distribution Hub

It was a peak Monday morning in late November. Throughput was at 140% capacity, and the floor was congested with unslotted pallets.

02. Incident

The Forklift Tip

A driver attempted a sharp turn to avoid a pallet, leading to a lateral tip-over. Fortunately, the overhead guard prevented injury.

03. Root Cause

Beyond Speed

Investigation found the primary cause wasn't speed, but the failure to manage floor congestion during peak demand periods.
