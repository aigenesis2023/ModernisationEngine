Hierarchy of Controls

1. Elimination expand_more
Physically remove the hazard. The most effective method of control.
2. Substitution expand_more
Replace the hazard with something less dangerous.
3. Engineering expand_more
Isolate people from the hazard through physical barriers or ventilation.
4. Administrative expand_more
Change the way people work through training and procedures.
5. PPE expand_more
Protect the worker with Personal Protective Equipment. The last line of defense.
