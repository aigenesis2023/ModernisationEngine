Introduction to FinTech: A comprehensive exploration of how technology is transforming financial services. Covers the evolution of digital payments and mobile banking, blockchain and cryptocurrency fundamentals, AI and machine learning in lending and fraud detection, open banking and API-driven ecosystems, regulatory technology (RegTech) and compliance automation, robo-advisors and democratised investing, and the future of embedded finance. Target audience: business professionals, finance teams, and technology leaders seeking to understand the FinTech landscape and its impact on traditional banking. Intermediate level, approximately 45 minutes.
