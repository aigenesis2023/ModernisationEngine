How to Be More Social

A practical course on building social confidence and meaningful connections. Covers overcoming social anxiety, starting conversations with strangers, reading social cues and body language, building and maintaining friendships, networking effectively, navigating group dynamics, being a better listener, and developing authentic charisma. The course should feel encouraging and actionable — not clinical or patronising — with real-world scenarios, self-reflection moments, and techniques people can practise immediately.
