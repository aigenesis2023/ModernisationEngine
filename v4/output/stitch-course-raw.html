Introduction

Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV)

100% electric. No combustion engine. These vehicles rely entirely on massive high-voltage battery packs and one or more electric motors. They represent the highest energy density hazard in the fleet.

Severity of Shock

AC is generally more dangerous than DC because it causes muscle tetany (the "can't let go" effect). However, the DC batteries in EVs can discharge enormous current instantaneously, leading to severe thermal burns and electrolysis of the blood.

warning CRITICAL WARNING

Never touch orange cables or damaged battery packs without appropriate Class 0 rated PPE.
